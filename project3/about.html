Duck Dash

High Concept

A endless runner featuring quick reflexy arcadey swaying left right and center staying the right lane to survive!

Genre

Endless Runner, Arcade?

Platform

Desktop-only

Story

I want to make this one a little abstract, you are a duck! They're easy to draw and cute... but more importantly you need to survive the onslaught of bread being thrown at you from off screen and stay on-screen!

Aesthetic

I plan to utilize hand drawn/pixel-art graphics in my game aiming for more of a sketchy and crude style

Gameplay

Mechanics

Players can move between the lanes, or left or right
Players can also choose to eat an oncoming piece of bread every now and then

Controls

WASD to move (only A and D will move during gameplay)
Space to eat bread

On boarding

Controls on main menu

Learning

The player will need good hand-eye coordination and learn the timing of the rapidly speeding up bread and when to eat and not

Documentation

The process for my project was to mostly use the basic example used in the circle-blast HW assignment and reverse engineer it into doing what I wanted, which ended up becoming more and more of me doing my own thing pretty differently besides mb some functions like animate or spritesheets and collision.
In terms of meeting the requirements: There are no errors and I used PIXI, the game is easy to play and takes little explaining, and is pretty robust!
I worked a lot on the design, there is some sound to give feedback and I've created all my own assets!
There are no CSS or HTML5 errors that appear when I check! My code is well commented and not written efficiently and fit code conventions
